Feature 1 of 6
Search & compare prices
Two ways to find and compare prices instantly — type a name or scan a barcode. Both give you the same powerful results.
⌨️ Option A — Type the item name
1
Tap the search bar at the top of the screen
Type any product name, brand, or category — "Nike Air Max," "whole milk," "Dyson vacuum," anything you want to buy.
2
Press Search and see top 10 results instantly
Results are ranked by true total cost — item price plus tax, shipping, and any fees — so you always see the real price.
📷 Option B — Scan the barcode (fastest!)
1
Tap the camera icon next to the search bar
The barcode scanner opens instantly using your phone's camera. Works on any product in any store.
2
Point at the barcode — results appear in 2 seconds
No typing needed. Scan the barcode on the shelf or product and PriceWatch instantly finds every store that sells it cheaper.
3
See the 12-month price history for that exact item
Know instantly if today's price is a deal, normal, or inflated — before you put it in your cart.
Both options then show the same results — top 10 prices ranked cheapest to most expensive across every store, website, and delivery service near you.

Grocery list comparison
