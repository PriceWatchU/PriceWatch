⚙️ Owner Setup Required — Connect Your Stripe Account

  1. Go to stripe.com and create a free account
  2. In your Stripe dashboard go to Developers → API Keys
  3. Copy your Publishable key (starts with pk_live_ for real payments or pk_test_ for testing)
  4. Open this file in a text editor, find YOUR_STRIPE_PUBLISHABLE_KEY below and replace it with your key
  5. In Stripe dashboard go to Products and create 4 products: Pro Monthly ($4.99), Pro Yearly ($25), Family Monthly ($7.99), Family Yearly ($39.50)
  6. Copy each product's Price ID (starts with price_) and replace the 4 placeholders below
  7. Connect your Mercury bank account in Stripe under Settings → Bank Account
  8. Delete or hide this yellow box once everything is set up
